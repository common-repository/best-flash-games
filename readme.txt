== Description ==
Best Flash Games  brings the best Games for your Site. The Content are hosted externaly and provides always best and new Games. When you click a game, it pops up in it's own menu free browser window. The Games are updated frequently. So your visitors play the latest games. No Traffic for your Site (Games are hosted by Game44.net). Games loades very quickly (Stand alone Server). No Ads on your Site. It is simple to install. Just download and enjoy it. Have a lot of Fun..!.

Over 350 online flash Games.
Best and new Games.
No traffic for your Site.
Quick Loads of Games (Stand alone Server).
Simple to install.

== Installation ==

1. Place the "bestflashgames.php" file in your wp-content/plugins folder
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Make a new page, type the following string:   \#bestflashgames

where you want the game list and play window to appear.

4. Enjoy! Read the plugin text for the remaining notes.
